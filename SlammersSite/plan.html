CP1406 - Assignment 1 - Project Plan Name: Slammers Beach Volleyball

Goals

To increase the amount of weekly participants from 100 to at least 150. Attracting a younger audience of 15-30 year-olds is also important to the club. The website should promote volleyball as a fun family sport.

Success Evaluation

The amount of people signing up due to the site can be determined by how many people use the sign-up form on the website. Alternatively if they are signing up at the club they can fill in a section asking “where did you hear about us?” which would determine if they found the club via the website.

Target Audience

The target audience of this site is predominately 15-30 year-olds as this an age range that is not currently marketed towards. However the site can also provide an overall increase in popularity regardless of age group.
This goal will be achieved by making an accessible website that appeals towards a younger crowd.
